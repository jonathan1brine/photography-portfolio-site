I'm a first-year Software Engineering student at the University of Adelaide. Photography is a side hobby — I shoot travel, street, and landscape, mostly just to document places and moments I'd otherwise forget.

I focus on quiet, everyday things: the light in a room, a view from a trail, the feeling of somewhere new. Nothing too staged.
